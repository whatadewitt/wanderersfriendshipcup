Visiting Fredericton

Spend your summer on the banks of Atlantic Canada’s Riverfront Capital!

Unique culinary experiences, one of a kind shopping opportunities, world-class art, and more await you in New Brunswick’s capital city. From indoor experiences at one of our many great music venues, to an outdoor experience you’ll never forget, we’ve got it all in store for you. No matter your interests, you will find something to do in Fredericton – and we’re here to help!
