Who Are We?

We Are The Wanderers... Wander far... Wander near...

The FDSA Wanderers Senior Men's Team

The Wanderers formed in 2002 as group of young, community-minded soccer players who wanted a place to play and a club atmosphere in which to have fun. Our team now consists of over 50 current players from around the Fredericton area, and an even larger number of Wanderers alumni who remain loyal to our club. We boast of a 2013 Challenge Cup winning “Senior A” team that played in the NBPSL  as well as a second division team that also captured its respective provincial title in 2012 and 2013 as well as winning multiple F.S.S.L. league and playoff titles over the years. Our philosophy is to strive for excellence in both sport and community involvement, and we are proud to announce ourselves as "Your Team in the Fredericton Community".

After operating independently for 12 years, the Wanderers, in 2013 were proud to partner with the Fredericton District Soccer Association and support all the work that the FDSA does to promote positive youth development in the capital region.

The Wanderers Development Group

...committed to youth development and community enhancement through sport.

In 2013, a group of 6 has been soccer players (Now called The Wanderers Development Group) wanted to give back, not only to our local soccer community but to a greater cause in The Gambia. We had come in contact to a dedicated group of volunteers in Brikama who were committed to youth development and community improvement through sport. It was impossible for us to not be inspired by the work these volunteers were doing in their community and so the two groups decided to create sister tournaments to be run in Fredericton, NB and Brikama in the Gambia called the Wanderers Friendship Cup.

Six years later we are both still running our concurrent soccer tournaments. Last year’s tournament in Brikama hosted 20 football academies and four schools participated in the girls category. Back here in Fredericton we hosted 20 boys and girls team at the U12 Division 1 level. The Organizing Committees on both sides of the Ocean aim to ensure that the Friendship Cup presents an opportunity for friendly competition - and we hope that through this - coaches and parents will see their children grow as players, teammates, and leaders. In addition to playing and competing (win or lose), we also place an enormous value on knowing that friendships grow throughout the weekend, and frankly across the world.

Through funds raised organizing our local tournament, we have been able to send over $14,000 in financial assistance to help our friends run their tournament. This would not be possible without our two main tournament sponsors, the Fredericton Inn and all four of the Fredericton area Y’s men clubs. We have also worked with our local soccer association, The FDSA, to send lightly used jerseys and balls.
